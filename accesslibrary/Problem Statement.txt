Problem Statement :

A Company named "LMS" is in the business of renting Notes, Books, Magazines, Daily or Weekly News Paper & CDs. 
Notes & Books are sales tax exempt, and CDs and Magazines have a sales tax of 10%.
CDs can be imported and attracts an import tax of 5%.

Write a renting basket program, which will calculate extended price(qty * (unit_price + tax) * num_of_days) inclusive of tax for each
item in the basket, total taxes and grand total.
